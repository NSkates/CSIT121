This section features labs that cover the fundamentals of HTML and CSS. That being said, the labs are generally easy. Due to the labs (especially the early ones) generally lacking much structure and content, I set each lab to be viewed through the <iframe>. Please enjoy previewing each lab and observing the difficulty increasing as you scroll through this page. These labs were done in the first half of the semester.

DISCLAIMER: There is a hyperlink showcased in three of these frames that leads to an external website. Please do not click the hyperlink inside the frames or else the frame will show an error. If you do click it by accident though, it's easy to fix. Just refresh this page! If you want to access the website I claimed to have helped me so much in these pages, please click the hyperlink embedded in this paragraph!

That being said, fun fact: frames leading to errors upon clicking hyperlinks is pretty normal. This happens for the following reasons:

  • Many big websites (such as Kahn Academy) disabled the ability to make their websites functional inside <iframe>!
  • To prevent hackers from abusing this tag
  • Some companies have policies that prevent embedding their content as a whole
